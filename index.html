new dynamic island

At this time, the presence of a new tool in the newly introduced iPhone 14 Pro and Pro Max is more than well known, that's right, we are talking about the new Dynamic Island or Dynamic Island technology.

Basically, this feature is presented as the replacement of the famous notch on the front camera, thus achieving what Apple was asking so much to remove that notch and now, this new technology will take place in that area and today we will be delving into what it is. and what is it for

Well, Dynamic Island is the name given to a new interaction bar, so to speak, which is located at the top of the screen, right where the front camera is. There, various information about calls, music, notifications, turn-by-turn navigation, among other controls and additional data will be displayed.
